Features

Simple. Powerful. Always awake.

⚡

Lid-Close Safe

The ONLY method that truly prevents sleep on Apple Silicon Macs. Even with the lid closed.

♾️

Forever Mode

No timer. No limits. Stays awake until you manually stop. Perfect for overnight runs.

🔄

Auto-Restore

Set a timer, forget about it. Sleep automatically re-enables when time's up.

🔐

No Password Prompts

Install the helper once. Done forever. No "enter your password" every time.

🪄

Menu Bar App

Lives in your tray. Minimal footprint. Always accessible, never in your way.

⏱️

Quick Presets

30min, 1h, 2h, 4h, 8h, 12h. One click. That's it.
